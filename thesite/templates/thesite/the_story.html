Our goal

Our goal is a conversation between consumers and producers that is accessible through an easy to use app. We want to be a source of information leading to the evolution of our food chain how we want.

Our Beliefs

We believe in sustainable production and farming that is independently verified. This extends beyond marketing gimmicks and meaningless certifications. People should feel confident that their purchases using green technology, that the workers are treated fairly and that they are supporting something that they believe in. This knowledge should be easily available to help us make informed decisions with our spending power.

We can do better.

How can you help?

Do you work on a farm, in a super market or for a producer. Are you a farm, producers or retailer? We want to hear from you. You can become a member and submit anything from a comment, photo or full report. We constantly receiving new information and updating our database to reflect the latest information.
